Avocado and Tuna Rice Bowl

Description

Marinated sashimi tuna and avocado over rice

Ingredients

  • 2 servings of rice
  • 200g of sashimi-grade tuna (maguro)
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 Tb each of soy sauce, mirin, and toasted sesame oil
  • wasabi, nori, and crispy chili oil to taste

Instructions

  1. Set rice in rice cooker with listed amount of water
  2. Mix soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil in a bowl
  3. Slice tuna into 1 inch cubes, add to step one bowl and let sit for 15-30 minutes
  4. Cut avocado into 1 inch cubes
  5. Add rice to serving bowls, then add avocado and tuna
  6. Add garnishes to taste as desired and serve
