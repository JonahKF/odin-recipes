Mashed Potatoes

Description

Creamy mashed potatoes with garlic and other aromatics

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs potatoes
  • 1/2 cup plus 2 Tbs unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 crushed garlic cloves
  • Bay leaf, thyme, chives etc. to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel potatoes before boiling in generously salted water for 15-20 minutes
  2. Drain and run potatoes through a ricer while still hot
  3. Melt unsalted butter in saucepan on medium-low heat
  4. Add in 4 cloves of lightly crushed skin-on garlic, add in whole milk and heavy cream, season with salt and whisk together
  5. Add desired aromatics
  6. Steep liquid for 10 minutes
  7. Strain, then toss with potatoes
