Pasta with Sausage and Tomatoes

Description

Pasta in a tomato sauce made with sausages and rosemary

Ingredients

  • 1lb Pasta
  • 1/2 lb Italian pork sausage
  • 2 Tbs butter
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 1/2 lbs fresh plum tomatoes, peeled and seeded
  • pinch of red pepper flakes to taste
  • salt
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 Tb flat leaf parsley
  • 1/4 cup freshly frated parmigiano-reggiano cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil sausages in water for 2-3 minutes, then remove and cut into rings
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil
  3. Melt butter in large saucepan, then add sausage and cook until browned
  4. Add rosemary, tomatoes, and 2 Tbs of water, cook for 5 minutes
  5. When water in pot is boiling, generously salt and add pasta, stirring well
  6. Add red pepper flakes to sauce, then cream and parsely, then stir until reduced by half
  7. Drain al-dente pasta and add to sauce w/ cheese and some reserved pasta water
